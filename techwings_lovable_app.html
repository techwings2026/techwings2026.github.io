01 Mission

We don't build drones.
We build engineers.

Tech Wings is a student-led drone engineering team driven by curiosity, creativity, and teamwork. We design, develop, and improve drones by combining engineering principles with hands-on learning. Every project helps us gain new skills, solve real-world challenges, and push our ideas further.

Our mission is to create innovative and reliable drone solutions while growing as future engineers. From design and prototyping to testing and continuous improvement, we believe every flight is an opportunity to learn.

Together, we are building more than drones — we are building experience, confidence, and the future of aviation.
